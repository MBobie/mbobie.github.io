Who I am

AI researcher.
Engineer. Founder.

Manuel Bobie Amankwatia

I am a Senior AI/ML Engineer & Data Scientist and PMP®-certified project manager with deep expertise in designing intelligent systems that remain reliable under real-world conditions — noisy data, low-resource environments, and production-scale pressure.

My research challenges a central assumption in AI: that higher benchmark accuracy reflects genuine progress. I study reliability and representation theory in neural NLP, formalising the Golden Draw Effect and using functional decomposition to explain how hybrid representations govern robustness.

As Founder & Chief AI Officer of DataMastaros Limited, I translate AI research into practical solutions for businesses across Ghana and beyond. I have presented at Indaba X, Deep Learning Indaba, and SPIE, and serve as a peer reviewer for EMNLP MRL 2025 and SACAIR 2024.
